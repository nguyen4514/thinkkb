Configuration & Setup

Please follow these steps to initialize the documentation engine:

1. API Credentials

Acquire an API key from Google AI Studio (Gemini) or Groq Console.

2. Extension Linking

Open ThinkKB settings and paste your key into the designated field. Select your preferred model (e.g., Gemini 1.5 Flash) and click "Save".

3. Execution

Click "Start Recording" before beginning your workflow. When finished, click "Stop". ThinkKB will capture the final state and compile the KB draft.
