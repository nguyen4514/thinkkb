Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: All screen captures and workflow logs are stored locally in your browser's sandbox. Data is only transmitted to your chosen AI provider for text generation.

Q: Do I need a paid subscription?

A: No. ThinkKB uses your own API keys. Google Gemini currently offers a free tier that works perfectly for this application.

Q: Why does it take a final screenshot?

A: The "Final Snap" ensures that your documentation includes the confirmed "Success" or "Complete" screen of your workflow automatically.

Q: Is it compatible with all websites?

A: It works on most standard web applications. Some internal enterprise tools with strict security headers may require specific permissions.
